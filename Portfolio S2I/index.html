Ethic and Philosophy


I believe that work is an important part of a person's life, something useful for himself and the world, something that gives more meaning to our lives. However, although it is important, it must not exceed certain limits, otherwise it would destroy our enthusiasm, our passion and the possibility to develop.

Mutual respect is the basis of every reality, including the workplace. If that were not the case, the rest of those fine words like collaboration or communication, which are the foundation of a healthy workplace, would naturally fall apart.
